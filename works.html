REBRANDING OF 'NESCAFE'






In the Nescafe Rebranding Project, the aim was to give a modern touch to the original logo while keeping its core intact. By adding a graphic element, the logo now resembles a coffee mug at first glance. However, a closer look reveals that the mug cleverly forms the letters of the brand name: N inside the mug, E represented by coffee foam, S as the mug handle, C forming the saucer, A depicted as a coffee bean, F illustrated by steam, and E elegantly rendered with coffee foam. The design is showcased through a mockup featuring coffee pods and signage, providing a tangible representation of the revamped Nescafe brand identity.










EXHIBITION STALL DESIGN





The exhibition stall design crafted for 'Modern Properties,' a prominent real estate company in Kolkata, seamlessly integrates branding and property showcase, with one side adorned by logos of various property companies. Drawing inspiration from the elegance of red carpet backdrops, a sophisticated checkered design enhances the visual appeal. On the other side, captivating images of the latest properties offered by them and upcoming projects are showcased, creating an engaging experience for visitors.
